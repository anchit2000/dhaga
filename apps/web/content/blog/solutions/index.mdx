Every one of these jobs runs on relationships — and in every one, the network
that matters most is scattered across business cards, half-remembered
conversations, LinkedIn, and a phone full of contacts with no context. Dhaga
turns that into a single private knowledge graph you can actually ask questions
of.

These guides are organized by the work you do. Each one walks through the daily
relationship problems of that profession and how Dhaga's core capabilities —
**capture anywhere, a private knowledge graph, natural-language recall, and
proactive intelligence** — map onto them.
