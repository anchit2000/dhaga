---
title: Custom entities
description: Model the non-person things that connect your people — gyms, schools, projects — as custom entity nodes with their own types.
---

Not everything in your network is a person. The gym where you know three people,
the school two contacts both attended, the project a handful of them worked on —
these are **entities**, and they connect people just like relationships do.

Manage them at **`/app/entities`**. Entities are grouped by **type**, and you
can define your own **custom node types** to match how you actually think about
your network.

![The entities page listing custom non-person nodes such as gyms, schools, and projects, grouped by type](/docs/guide/entities-list.png)

Entities appear on the [graph](/docs/guide/exploring-the-graph) alongside
people, so a shared gym or project becomes a visible thread between the people
attached to it. Use the Layers panel there to show or hide entity types when the
canvas gets busy.
