**`/app/graph`** renders your entire network on a single canvas. Every person
and connection you've captured is one node in a graph you can pan, zoom, and
interrogate.

![The Dhaga graph canvas showing the full network settled into a layout, with the warm-path panel in the top-left corner](/docs/guide/graph-overview.png)

## Find a warm path

The **Warm path** finder answers "how do I get introduced to this person?"
Enter a target and Dhaga renders the **intro chains** that connect you to them
through people you both know — for example *You → Bhavna Costa → Aarav Bose*.

![The warm-path panel listing three intro chains from you to a target person through mutual connections](/docs/guide/graph-warm-path.png)

## Inspect a node

Click any node to open its **side panel**. It shows that person's edges — split
into **incoming** and **outgoing** — and lets you:

- **Add a relationship** to another person.
- **Open** their full profile page.
- **Isolate their circle** to focus on just their immediate connections.

![The graph with a node selected, its side panel showing incoming and outgoing edge counts, and the focused node's circle isolated](/docs/guide/graph-node-panel.png)

## Layers and controls

- The **Layers** panel toggles which node types and circles are drawn, so you
  can declutter a dense graph.
- **Zoom**, **fit**, and **reset** controls frame the view — fit to see
  everything, reset to return to the default layout.

Non-person nodes like gyms or projects show up here too — see
[Custom entities](/docs/guide/entities).
