**Dhaga** turns everyone you meet into a private knowledge graph you own. You
capture a person once — from a card photo, a pasted email signature, a voice
note — and Dhaga keeps the source as a receipt, extracts the facts, and wires
them into a graph you can search, ask questions of, and explore visually.

This guide walks through the app the way you actually use it. Each page is a
short "how to" for one part of the product.

## Your daily briefing

`/app` opens on the Home briefing — the short list of **threads to pull
today**: people worth reaching out to now, follow-ups you set, and the people
you touched most recently. Each row has a one-tap **Reached out** button so the
list stays honest. The capture dock sits at the bottom of every screen —
**Voice**, **Camera**, **Upload**, and **Capture** — so you can add someone the
moment you meet them.
